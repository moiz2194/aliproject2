What is Phlete?

Established in 2016, Phlete is a GPS-trackable mobile application connecting pre-screened drivers with vehicle owners who want to have their cars driven and delivered to and from maintenance appointments.

Benefits of Phlete

  • As an independent contractor, you determine your own schedule and earn as often as you want to.
  • The Phlete platform finds your customers for you.
  • Because you are not earning with your personal vehicle, your profits are not impacted by fuel and vehicle maintenance costs.
  • Signing up early puts you in position to get a greater share of earnings before more drivers join.
  • Other Phlete drivers will drive you in between your appointments.
